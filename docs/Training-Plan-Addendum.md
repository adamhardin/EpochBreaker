# Training Plan Addendum: Creativity, Fun, and Expert Feedback

This addendum extends the training plan to ensure the expert agent demonstrates mastery in creativity, artistry, imaginative thinking, and fun-focused game evaluation. It adds new modules, assessments, and certification criteria that close the gaps identified in the review.

## Phase 1A: Creative Direction and Fun Validation

### Module 3A: Creative Direction, Fun, and Player Experience

**Topics**
- Fun hypotheses and player emotion goals
- Playtest design (qualitative, observational, think-aloud)
- Delight, surprise, and pacing craftsmanship
- Novelty vs. familiarity balance for retro-inspired play
- Clear articulation of taste, intent, and tradeoffs

**Assessments**
- 3A.1: Fun Hypothesis and Emotional Pillars
- 3A.2: Qualitative Playtest Plan and Execution
- 3A.3: Delight Moment Map and Iteration Log
- 3A.4: Player Experience Rubric and Calibration
- Capstone: Fun Validation Report (pre- and post-iteration)

---

## Phase 2A: Imagination and Outside-the-Box Design

### Module 4A: Ideation, Innovation, and Creative Risk

**Topics**
- Divergent ideation methods (morphological matrix, constraints-based creativity)
- Mechanic mashups and novelty scaffolding
- Thematic reinterpretation of classic mechanics
- Prototyping for creativity and surprise
- Risk assessment and creative learning loops

**Assessments**
- 4A.1: Divergent Mechanics Sprint (10 ideas)
- 4A.2: Mechanic Mashup Challenge (3 concepts, 1 selected)
- 4A.3: Novelty Prototype with Risk Report
- 4A.4: Creative Constraint Puzzle (ship quality under odd constraints)
- Capstone: Innovation Brief and Prototype Review

---

## Phase 2B: Narrative, Theme, and Artistic Cohesion

### Module 6A: Theme, Worldbuilding, and Artistic Intent

**Topics**
- Theme and tone definition for retro worlds
- Visual and audio motif consistency
- Character and enemy silhouette language
- Environmental storytelling in side-scrollers
- Story beats aligned with difficulty and reward pacing

**Assessments**
- 6A.1: Theme and Tone One-Pager
- 6A.2: Motif and Symbolism Map
- 6A.3: Environmental Storytelling Pass
- 6A.4: Aesthetic Cohesion Audit (art, audio, UX)
- Capstone: World and Theme Integration Deck

---

## Phase 3A: Expert Feedback and Critique Calibration

### Module 7A: Feedback Quality and Evaluation Standards

**Topics**
- Actionable critique frameworks (clarity, evidence, impact)
- Calibration against reference exemplars
- Bias control and blind review methods
- Feedback that preserves creative intent
- Prioritization (what to fix now vs. later)

**Assessments**
- 7A.1: Reference Critique Calibration (5 gold-standard levels)
- 7A.2: Actionable Feedback Rubric (pass threshold >= 4.0/5.0)
- 7A.3: Blind Review Exercise (identify issues without context)
- 7A.4: Creative Intent Preservation Test
- Capstone: Expert Feedback Portfolio

## New Assessment Criteria (Summarized)

### Assessment 3A.1: Fun Hypothesis and Emotional Pillars
**Objective**
Define the intended player emotions and how the design will produce them.

**Requirements**
- 3-5 emotional pillars (e.g., mastery, surprise, flow)
- Mapping of each pillar to concrete mechanics and moments
- Risks to fun and mitigation plan

**Deliverables**
```
fun_hypothesis.md
emotional_pillars_map.png
fun_risk_register.md
```

**Pass Threshold**: >= 85/100

---

### Assessment 3A.2: Qualitative Playtest Plan and Execution
**Objective**
Demonstrate the ability to validate fun through structured playtests.

**Requirements**
- 3 sessions, 20-30 minutes each
- Think-aloud protocol and observation notes
- 5+ actionable insights, each mapped to a design change

**Deliverables**
```
playtest_plan.md
playtest_notes.md
insight_to_change_log.md
```

**Pass Threshold**: >= 85/100

---

### Assessment 3A.3: Delight Moment Map and Iteration Log
**Objective**
Identify and intentionally design moments of surprise or delight.

**Requirements**
- 8+ moments mapped to levels, mechanics, or rewards
- Before/after iteration evidence (how delight improved)

**Deliverables**
```
delight_map.md
iteration_log.md
```

**Pass Threshold**: >= 80/100

---

### Assessment 3A.4: Player Experience Rubric and Calibration
**Objective**
Create a consistent rubric for evaluating player experience quality.

**Requirements**
- Rubric categories: fun, clarity, flow, fairness, surprise, comfort
- Calibration against 3 reference games

**Deliverables**
```
px_rubric.md
px_calibration_notes.md
```

**Pass Threshold**: average >= 4.0/5.0

## Addendum Success Criteria

- Pass all new modules with >= 85% overall average.
- Fun validation results show a positive delta between first and second iteration.
- Feedback portfolio includes at least 20 actionable, testable recommendations.
- Aesthetic cohesion audit passes without major conflicts.
